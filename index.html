About Me

I have recently received a Diploma of Information Technology from the 6 month coding bootcamp at Coderacademy in Brisbane. The Wotnot Media website was built for the final assessment at Coderacademy which was to build an app for a real world client, in this case a portfolio site for graphic design company. Tekmore:

https://glacial-castle-71209.herokuapp.com

Tekmore is the name of the two-sided marketplace app built for the Ruby on Rails assessment at Coderacadmey. In summary the app has a landing page, account sign up, login, edit and delete options, a listings page where users can view other users' listings or create, edit, or delete their own, a shopping cart, and a payment system implemented with Stripe.
